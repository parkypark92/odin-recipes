Turkey Nachos

Irresistably tasty, great for sharing!

Ingredients:

  • 500g turkey mince
  • 1 small red onion, chopped
  • Tin of black beans
  • Handful of plum tomatoes, chopped
  • Squirt of BBQ sauce
  • Taco seasoning
  • Tortilla chips
  • Salsa
  • Grated cheese

    • To serve:

  • Guacamole
  • Sour cream
  • Jalapenos

Method:

  1. Heat a bit of oil in a pan. Cook turkey, red onion and taco seasoning until browned.
  2. Add black beans, tomatoes, BBQ sauce and a large splash of water and cook few more minutes.
  3. Plate up tortilla chips topped with salsa and grated cheese and cook in oven until cheese is melted.
  4. Top with turkey meat, guacamole, sour cream and jalepenos.
  5. Get creative and add whatever other toppings you like!
