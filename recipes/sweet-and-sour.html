Sweet and Sour Chicken

A family favourite, packed with crispy chicken and juicy pineapple!

Ingredients:

  • 650g chicken thighs
  • 1 pineapple, chopped
  • 2 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 eggs
  • Flour

    • For sauce:

  • Quarter cup brown sugar
  • Half cup apple cider vinegar
  • Large squirt of ketchup
  • 4 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 4 minced garlic cloves

Method:

  1. Coat the chicken thighs in egg, then flour and shallow fry in oil until crispy. Set aside.
  2. Discard all but a tablespoon worth of oil and fry bell pepper, onion and pineapple for a few minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, whisk together sauce ingredients and add to pan when vegetables are softening.
  4. Cook until bubbling and return chicken to pan.
  5. Heat chicken through and serve with rice!
